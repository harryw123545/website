Journey Book

The brief for this project was to create a publication that covered the development and final outcomes of our first unit back at university. This covered three projects as well as an industry visit to Thinking Juice, a Bournemouth based design agency. This was my first attempted to make a book on this scale and I found it to be a very fun experience. I learned a lot about the planning and methods of book design during this project.
