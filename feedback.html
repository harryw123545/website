Visual Feedback Experiments

These are a series of visual feedback experiments that I conducted while making my music video. Visual feedback is a relatively straightforward process; you connect a camera to a screen so it displays the cameras output. You then point it at the screen, creating a hall of mirrors effect. Type can be incorporated by printing it onto acetate and sticking it in front of the screen. I went one further on this by running the cameras output through a VHS enhancer that I had modified. This causes the image on the screen to glitch sporadically. I am interested in creating more experiments with this in the future, particularly within an exhibition space.
