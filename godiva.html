Stories Through Chocolate

This is the result of a two week live brief with the design agency ‘Thinking Juice’. We were instructed to come up with an advertising campaign for Godiva, a luxury chocolate brand. My group’s idea was to tell “Stories through Chocolate”: packaging inspired by classic works of fiction. I looked at the origins of the Godiva legend, creating a design inspired by medieval art. I learned a lot about the design industry and how it operates during these two weeks.
