Copper Installation

This installation was the result of a group project during our first term back at university. Our brief was to create a “portrait of AUB”. We were encouraged to be experimental with our approach to how we visualise data. Our idea was to map the amount of pressure students feel in their lives: We conducted a survey asking what factors contribute to students feeling pressure (for example university deadlines, home life etc). We then visualised this data by applying pressure to sheets of copper.
