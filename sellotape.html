Sellotape Installation

This is part of an ongoing project between me and my friends where we create life-sized people out of sellotape, placing them anonymously in public spaces. Making one of them is quite a time consuming process, as you have to completely cover the model in tape several times before cutting them out and taping them back up again. The project has been featured on national news and we are currently planning our next installation for a homeless charity.
