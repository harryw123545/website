About

I like to be experimental in my design approach and am curious about learning new techniques. I believe that the design process is just as important as the final outcome. I am a lover of music and the cultures/art that comes with various genres, my work is often influenced by this.

Recently I have become interested in installation art and how I can affect the atmosphere of a place, with my latest project being inspired by Nam June Paik. I used analogue equipment to achieve a distinctly retro, psychedelic style, and I plan on using the skills I have gained from this project to make an installation in the future. If you would like to know more feel free to check out my blog.
